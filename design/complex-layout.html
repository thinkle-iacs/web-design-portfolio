Complex Layout

Experimentation

  • You create a layout which involves aligning items in at least 2 different ways (e.g. on a left-right and top-bottom axis).
  • You pay close attention to alignment and spacing, creating even spacings and alignments or breaking them deliberately for effect.
  • You structure your HTML and CSS in a way that makes your layout easy to adjust and understand, using features such as flexbox or CSS grid to lay out your page.
