CRAG: Simple Design

Experimentation

You use strong "CRAG" principles to design a simple stand-alone page (a simple standalone style homepage, card or advertisement).

  • Contrast: you set up strong contrasts in your design.
  • Repetition: you use repeated elements such as color, font, and sizing, to create a rhythm to your design.
  • Alignment: you set up clear, strong alignments in your design.
  • Grouping: you successfully group like items together.
