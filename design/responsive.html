Responsive

Experimentation

You create a responsive design that adjusts to different screens-sizes successfully.

  • Typography: You use font-sizes that are readable for each screen-size.
  • Layout: You adjust your layout appropriately to make it use the space of each screen size effectively.
  • Images: You make sure that your graphical elements look good on different screensizes and layouts.
  • Mobile/Tablet/Widescreen: you have clearly tested your design at at least three key widths.
